Bio

iLLann is a songstress based on Vancouver Island who uses a violin and loop pedal to weave hypnotic soundscapes full of cascading harmonies, organic beats, and haunting strings.

She grew up in the mountains of Northern BC, singing, fiddling, and studying classical violin from a young age. Gradually her intuition led her into the realms of alternative and trip hop, for she feels drawn to ambient dissonance; to psychedelic layers and velvety depths. Her music reflects the raw intensity and vividness of lucid dreams, early memories, and deep truths.

She draws creative inspiration from a wellspring of artists like Agnes Obel, Portishead, Massive Attack, Bonobo, Hannah Epperson, and Tom Yorke.

iLLann has recently begun facilitating sound healing journeys, where she incorporates singing bowls, harp, chimes, mantras, and poetry into the soundscape. She has collaborated with healers of various mediums on these journeys, and is very inspired to continue expanding on this offering
